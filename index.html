PAUL HEAD


Paul Head is a multidisciplinary artist based in New York. He creates olfactory artworks, often with performative happenings, and acts in numerous independent short films. He explores the sensuous, the wild, and the mythic aspects of being human. Paul graduated from NYU Tisch School of the Arts with a BFA in Film & Television.
